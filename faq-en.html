How can I register for Quran memorization classes?

You can easily register by contacting us via phone at 01023231780, through WhatsApp, or by visiting the academy in person. Our team will welcome you and conduct a simple level assessment test to place you in the appropriate class for your level[web:35].

What is the expected duration to memorize the entire Quran?

The duration varies based on several factors: the student's age, memorization ability, daily time dedicated to memorization, and the chosen package. On average, with consistent attendance and revision, the entire Quran can be memorized in 2 to 4 years for adults, and less for children[web:35][web:38].

Do the instructors hold Ijazah certifications in Quran?

Yes, all instructors at Dar Al-Hidaya Academy hold Ijazah certifications with connected chains (Sanad) to the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. We have more than 35 certified male and female instructors in various Qira'at and Tajweed.

Can I obtain an Ijazah certification in Quran?

Yes, we offer special programs to obtain Ijazah certification in Quran recitation with different narrations (Hafs, Warsh, Qalon, and others) as well as Ijazah in Tajweed. Complete memorization of the Quran and mastery of recitation and Tajweed are required[web:38].

What do Hajj and Umrah packages include?

Our packages include: round-trip airfare, accommodation in hotels near the Holy Mosques, transportation between sacred sites, daily meals, health insurance, guidance from a specialized religious guide, and a Hajj/Umrah kit. Details vary depending on the chosen package (Economy or VIP)[web:36].

When does registration open for the upcoming Hajj season?

Registration for Hajj typically opens 8-10 months before the Hajj date. Early registration is recommended to secure the appropriate package and better prices. For Umrah, registration is available year-round with weekly departures.

Is there medical support during the trip?

Yes, all our trips are fully health-insured, and we provide an accompanying medical team on Hajj trips, along with a mobile pharmacy and coordination with hospitals in the Holy Land for any emergency[web:36].

What payment methods are available?

We offer several payment methods: cash at the academy, bank transfer, Vodafone Cash, or credit cards. We also provide flexible payment plans for annual packages and Hajj trips.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for annual packages and Hajj & Umrah packages. The amount can be divided into 3 or 6 installments depending on the chosen package, with a small down payment upon registration.

Are there discounts for families or groups?

Yes! We offer special discounts for families (when registering more than two members from the same family) and group discounts (when registering 5 people or more). Discounts can reach up to 15-20% depending on the number.

What are the academy's operating hours?

The academy operates from Saturday to Thursday from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and on Friday from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Customer service is available 24/7 via phone and WhatsApp.

Is online learning available?

Yes, we offer online memorization programs via Zoom and Skype with high quality. You can have individual 1:1 sessions with certified instructors at flexible times that suit you, with the ability to record sessions for review[web:35][web:38].

How is student progress tracked?

We provide periodic reports (weekly or monthly depending on the package) detailing memorization level, recitation quality, attendance, and notes. We also offer an electronic tracking app for parents to monitor their children's progress in real-time[web:35][web:38].

Is there separation between males and females?

Yes, the academy maintains complete separation between males and females in classes and sessions. We have specialized female instructors for sisters and separate halls, while maintaining the same level of quality and service for everyone.
