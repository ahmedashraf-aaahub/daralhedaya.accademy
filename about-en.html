Our Founding Story

A journey that began with a dream and was realized by Allah's grace

In 2015, the journey of Dar Al-Hidaya Academy began with a simple dream: to be a bridge connecting people with the Book of Allah, and to facilitate the path of worship for Allah's guests.

We started with just one circle and ten students, but the passion for serving the Holy Quran and sincerity in work had the greatest impact on the Academy's growth and spread. Today, we serve more than 500 male and female students in memorization circles, and organize annual trips for more than 1,000 pilgrims and Umrah performers.

We are proud to have 25 certified male and female instructors with chains of narration connected to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, providing their educational services with sincerity and dedication. We also take pride in our partnerships with accredited religious institutions in Egypt and Saudi Arabia.

Our vision is clear: to be the leading beacon in teaching the Holy Quran and serving Allah's guests in the Arab world, with international standards combining authenticity and development.

Our Vision, Mission & Values

The principles we follow in serving the faith

Our Vision

To be the leading beacon in teaching the Holy Quran and serving Allah's guests in the Arab world, through solid academic curricula and international standards combining authenticity and development, contributing to building a conscious Quranic generation carrying the true message of Islam.

Our Mission

To provide distinguished Quranic education combining perfect memorization with deep understanding, organize accessible spiritual journeys for Hajj and Umrah, and offer comprehensive Islamic studies that build a balanced Muslim personality, committed to faith, aware of reality, and capable of serving the community.

  • Continuous development
