Quran Memorization

We offer specialized Quran memorization circles for all age groups:

  • Children circles (4-12 years): Interactive and engaging curriculum
  • Youth circles (13-25 years): Memorization with Tafseer studies
  • Adult circles (25+ years): Flexible programs suitable for work schedules
  • Male and female instructors certified with connected chains
  • Regular review and reinforcement of memorization
  • Graduation ceremonies and honoring memorizers
  • Certified certificates upon completion

Tajweed & Ijazah

Specialized programs to master Tajweed rules and obtain Ijazah:

  • Comprehensive theoretical study of Tajweed rules
  • Practical application on correct recitation
  • Ijazah in Hafs narration from Asim
  • Ijazah in the Ten Qira'at (for advanced students)
  • Connected chains to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
  • Certified certificates from authorized scholars
  • Individual follow-up for each student

Islamic Studies
